Inspiration

Over the course of the MLH Fellowship, all of us have faced the issue of merging stack PRs.It has been mildly annoying how the cascading commits has hampered workflow and we wanted tobuild a tool that can merge and rebase all of these commits on the branch/commit we desire. And hence, stack attack was born.
